Summary.

performs a rotation of the polygon specified by angle arround specified center.

Function usage:

[ rotPol ] = rotatePolygon( vertex, center, angle )

Description:

Performs a rotation of the given polygon (specified by its vertices coordinates either
geographical, UTM or another cartesian system) by angle arround specified center.

I/O parameters:

Input parameters: 

 Here are the parameters used as inputs for this function:
  
      
  • [float] vertex: matrix containing the coordinates of the polygon's vertices 'closed'
(the last coordinate of the vertices and the first are the same one) {m or deg.}
    • 
  
      
  • [float] center: vector containing the coordinates of the polygon's centroid {m or
deg.}
    • 
  
      
  • [float] angle: angle of rotation of the polygon {deg}

    •

    Output parameters: 

     Here are the parameters used as inputs for this function:
  
      
  • [float]  rotPol:  coordinates of the rotated polygon {m or deg.}
